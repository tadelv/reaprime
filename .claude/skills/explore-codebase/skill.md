## Explore Codebase

Use the knowledge graph to explore and understand the codebase.

### Steps

1. Run `get_architecture_overview` for high-level structure.
2. Use `semantic_search_nodes` to find specific functions or classes.
3. Use `query_graph` with `callers_of`, `callees_of`, `imports_of` to trace relationships.
4. Use `get_affected_flows` to understand execution paths.
